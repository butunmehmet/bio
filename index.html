Mehmet Bütün

Physics Researcher | Nanophotonics and Laser Fabrication

I’m a Master’s student in Physics advised by Prof. Onur Tokel at Bilkent University. I conduct my research at the Photonic Devices Laboratory, where I focus on developing advanced laser-material interaction techniques and nanophotonic devices. During my undergraduate years, I worked extensively on laser-written metasurfaces and multi-level diffraction gratings under the same mentorship.

My current research addresses the increasing demand for innovative and scalable photonics solutions by exploring novel laser nano-patterning methods. I combine numerical modeling with experimental work to contribute to next-generation optical systems.
