Graduate Researcher | Nanophotonics and Photonic devices

I’m a Master’s student in Physics department under supervision of Prof. Onur Tokel at Bilkent University. My research focuses on developing innovative laser-material interaction techniques and silicon photonic devices. I combine theoretical modeling with experimental methods to contribute to next-generation optical systems. My journey in nanophotonics has been marked by impactful publications and collaborations through my 5 years in Photonic Devices Laboratory.

About

I am deeply passionate about pushing the boundaries of photonics to address modern technological challenges. My work spans advanced laser nano-patterning, nanophotonics and metasurface fabrication and design, and integrated photonics.
